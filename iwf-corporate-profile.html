Corporate Profile

Key Personnel

Ernie Calderin

General Manager

Born and raised in Miami, Ernie Calderin founded IWF Construction & Glazing after successful stints as a manager of new project development and project coordinator at various highly-respected South Florida businesses. As a project coordinator he has overseen projects with budgets ranging from $500,000 to over $5 million.

Safety

I.W.F. Construction & Glazing is a leader when it comes to safety. All our projects are staffed with OSHA certified professionals. Our field supervisors keep daily safety logs and all our staff is routinely educated on all OSHA standards. We pride ourselves on keeping a standard of Safety like no other.
