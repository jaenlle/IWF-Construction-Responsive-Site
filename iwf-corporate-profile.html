Key Personnel

Ernesto "Ernie" Calderin

General Manager

Born and raised in Miami, Ernie Calderin founded IWF Construction & Glazing after successful stints as a manager of new project development and project coordinator at several South Florida businesses. As a project coordinator he has overseen projects with budgets ranging from $500,000 to over $5 million.

Corporate Mission

IWF Construction & Glazing was founded on the principle of providing the best service to our clients. We pride ourselves in the quality of our staff and the quality of our work. Safety

I.W.F. Construction & Glazing is a leader when it comes to safety. All our projects are staffed with OSHA certified professionals. Our field supervisors keep daily safety logs and all our staff is routinely educated on all OSHA standards. We pride ourselves on keeping a standard of Safety like no other.
